About Us

Introduction

Who Are We And What We Do?

KH3 is Ghana’s leading Construction Project Management Firm with a focus on Interior Design and Fit-out. Founded in 2014 by a highly motivated team, KH3 is committed to being the standard of business excellence in Africa.

Our turnkey approach provides our clients with a single point of contact. This liberates them to refocus on their core business. Results: Exceptional projects completed within budget and on time!

Our forward-thinking, highly sought-after network of professionals, artisans, suppliers, and subcontractors make up the powerhouse that is the KH3 team.

Guided by our core values of Beauty, Integrity, Loyalty, Diligence, Excellence, Resilience, and Studiousness, our team has executed over 20,000 square meters of office space for international and national brands such as IBM, Nestle, and Enterprise Group Limited.
